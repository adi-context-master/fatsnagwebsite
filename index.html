FOUNDER STORY

A problem I lived.

I spent years with a camera kit in a bag doing nothing. I knew creators nearby who'd have killed to use it. I knew other owners with the same problem. The gear existed. The need existed. No good way to connect the two.

Then I realised it wasn't just gear. Producers were running whole productions on spreadsheets and WhatsApp. Crew were chasing payments. Post-production was a black hole.

So we built the whole thing.

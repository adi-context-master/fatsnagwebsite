Why we built this

A problem I lived.

A few years ago I found myself staring at a camera kit I'd spent serious money on — sitting in a bag, doing absolutely nothing for months on end.

"The gear existed. The need existed. There was just no good way to connect the two — safely, simply, and without the risk."

I knew there were creators nearby who would have loved to use it. And I knew gear owners like me were sitting on thousands of dollars of idle equipment with no easy way to make it work for them.

That's the gap Fat Snag fills. An AI-powered, fully insured marketplace where creators access professional gear and gear owners finally put their kit to work. Peer-to-peer, intelligently matched, and covered from the moment the booking is confirmed.
